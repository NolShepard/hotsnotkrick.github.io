Driving

These are my latest important thoughts

  1. Proper setup of arm pit, knee caps, and middle foot all on same verticle line
  2. Your right hand on the club should be directly down from the eyes
  3. On the backswing, when the club reaches parrallel to the ground the face should be pointing directly up
  4. Balance!
  5. Slow tempo back
  6. For accuracy, only swing back till the left arm is parrallel to the ground
  7. For distance, increase turn
